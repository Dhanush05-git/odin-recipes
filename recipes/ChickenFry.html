Chicken Fry

Description

Chicken fry is a popular, flavor-packed dish featuring succulent pieces of chicken seasoned with bold spices and fried to perfection. It is known for its crispy exterior and juicy, tender interior.

This dish offers a delightful balance of heat and savory notes, often garnished with fresh aromatics, making it a versatile favorite that works perfectly as a standalone snack or a side dish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (Boneless/Bone-in)
  • Onions
  • Chilli
  • Oil
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Curry Leaves
  • Salt
  • Masala powder (Garam/Chicken Masala)

Steps

  1. Marinate Chicken: Coat the chicken pieces with salt, masala powder, and ginger-garlic paste. Let it sit for 20 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  2. Sauté Aromatics: Heat oil in a pan. Add the onions, green chillies, and curry leaves. Sauté until the onions are golden brown.
  3. Sear the Chicken: Add the marinated chicken to the pan. Cook on high heat for a few minutes to seal in the juices.
  4. Cook Thoroughly: Lower the heat, cover the pan, and let the chicken cook in its own juices until tender and fully cooked.
  5. Fry for Crispiness: Remove the lid and increase the heat. Stir-fry the chicken until the moisture evaporates and the pieces turn dark brown and slightly crispy.
  6. Final Garnish: Adjust the salt if needed and give it a final toss with a sprinkle of fresh masala powder before serving.
