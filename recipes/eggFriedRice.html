Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg fried rice is a beloved, comforting staple characterized by individual, fluffy grains of rice tossed over high heat, creating a slightly smoky aroma and savory flavor profile.

It boasts a satisfying texture contrast, featuring delicate, scrambled egg bits mixed with tender rice, often lightly coated in a savory, umami-packed sauce, resulting in a golden-hued appearance

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Chilli
  • Oil
  • Eggs
  • Veggies
  • Salt
  • Masala powder
  • Boiled (Normal/Basmati)

Steps

  1. Prep the Eggs: Heat a little oil in a pan, crack the eggs in, add a pinch of salt, and scramble them until just cooked. Remove and set aside.
  2. Sauté Aromatics: Add more oil to the same pan. Sauté the onions and chilli until the onions turn translucent.
  3. Cook Veggies: Toss in your veggies and stir-fry on high heat for a few minutes until they are tender but still crunchy.
  4. Season: Stir in the masala powder and salt, ensuring the spices are well-distributed through the vegetables.
  5. Combine Rice: Add the boiled rice to the pan. Toss everything together gently so the grains don't break but are evenly coated with the spices.
  6. Final Mix: Fold the scrambled eggs back into the rice. Stir-fry for another minute until everything is piping hot.
